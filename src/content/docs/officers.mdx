## Executive Committee

The Executive Committee is responsible for the day-to-day operations of the House of Representatives and for implementing the decisions made by the general body.

## Committee Chairpersons

Committee Chairpersons lead specialized groups focusing on specific areas of student governance, ensuring that all aspects of student life are addressed effectively.

## Elections

Officers are elected annually by the student body. Elections are typically held in March, with new officers taking office at the beginning of the academic year.
